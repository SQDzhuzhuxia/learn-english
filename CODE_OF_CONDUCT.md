# Code of Conduct

[中文](#中文) · [English](#english)

## 中文

Learn English 希望成为一个认真、友善、长期主义的开源学习社区。

请做到：

- 尊重不同英语水平、背景和学习目标的人。
- 讨论问题时聚焦事实、代码、学习方法和用户体验。
- 给反馈时具体、建设性、可执行。
- 不嘲笑学习者的口音、语法错误、表达错误或移民经历。
- 不发布歧视、骚扰、攻击、色情、仇恨或泄露隐私的内容。

维护者可以对违反社区氛围的行为进行提醒、隐藏内容、关闭讨论或限制参与。

## English

Learn English aims to be a serious, kind, and long-term open-source learning community.

Please:

- Respect people with different English levels, backgrounds, and goals.
- Keep discussions focused on facts, code, learning methods, and user experience.
- Give feedback that is specific, constructive, and actionable.
- Do not mock learners for accents, grammar mistakes, expression mistakes, or immigration experiences.
- Do not post discriminatory, harassing, abusive, sexual, hateful, or privacy-violating content.

Maintainers may warn, hide content, close discussions, or limit participation when behavior harms the community.
